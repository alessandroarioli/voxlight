# Voxlight — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 10, 2026**

Voxlight reads text you select on a web page out loud, highlighting words as they are spoken.

## Data collection

Voxlight collects **no data**. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted.
- No browsing history, page content, or selected text ever leaves your device.
- No analytics, tracking, cookies, or telemetry of any kind.
- No accounts, no sign-in.

## How your text is processed

Text-to-speech is performed entirely **on your device** by your browser's built-in speech engine (Web Speech API). The text you select is never sent to any server.

## Settings storage

Your preferences (chosen voice, speed, pitch) are stored using Chrome's `storage.sync` API. This data stays within your own browser profile (and syncs across your own devices if you have Chrome sync enabled). The developer has no access to it.

## Permissions

- **activeTab / scripting** — used only when you explicitly invoke Voxlight (context menu or keyboard shortcut) to read your selection on the current tab.
- **contextMenus** — adds the "Read aloud with Voxlight" right-click menu item.
- **storage** — saves your voice/speed/pitch preferences.
